# Kendall 

### Short Biography
Kendall works as a community manager for an international NGO in Netherlands. Kendall has no knowledge about open source, citizen science, and open science. They have heard about participatory projects before but never particiaptes. They heard  AutSPACEs from Autistica through a forum. 

Kendall wants to learn from the experiences of autistic individuals so they pitch for policy changes in their organization that will inform building and interior design, working policies, and equipment support for their employees. 

### Goals and Motivations
| **Goals** | **Motivations** | 
| -------- | -------- |
